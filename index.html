Working Process

Goals and Motivation

The goal of DJTable is to make a collaborative tabletop music experience for users through soothing music and synesthetic visual graphics.

Music has been proposed as psychological support for patients with low- and medium-grade depression. Therefore, the motivation of DJTable is to enable music creation even for people who cannot play any instruments.

Graphics

DJTable is an abstract themed experience. The graphics are consist of two parts:

a) For the tabletop graphics each physical objects has particles around them. Each object has a specific colored particle sysrtem. There is a dial cirle around the objects. By rotating the objects the color nuances changes and you get different tracks.

b) A standalone space-themed visual experience will be presented for the audience who are not interacting with the table. The visual integrates with the music and creates pleasing visual experience.

Interaction

The interaction with the tabletop is done through physical objects, called fiducials. Each of the fiducials represents a specific instrument. A trackable tag is attached underneath.

3D Models

To create a representation of each instrument, 3D physical objects were created. The final shape of these physical objects was decidedthrough gathering the result of a survey that was posted on AGI19 Facebook group.
