Working Process

Goals and Motivation

The goal of DJTable is to make a collaborative tabletop music experience for users through soothing music and synesthetic visual graphics.

Music has been proposed as psychological support for patients with low- and medium-grade depression. Therefore, the motivation of DJTable is to enable music creation even for people who cannot play any instruments.

Graphics

The graphics for DJTable has an abstract theme. It consists of two parts:

a) The tabletop graphics, where each physical objects has particles around them. Each object has a specific color. The color nuances changes depending on different parameters.

b) A standalone space-themed visual experience for the audience. It integrates with the music and creates a pleasing visual experience.

Interaction

The interaction with the tabletop is done through physical objects, called fiducials. Each of the fiducials represents a specific instrument. It has a trackable marker underneath.

3D Models

To create a representation of each instrument, 3D physical objects were created. The final shape of these physical objects was decidedthrough gathering the result of a survey that was posted on AGI19 Facebook group.
